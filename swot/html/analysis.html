SWOT Analysis

Internal Strengths Weaknesses

  • Well received menu

    • Different categories of food served that provide for multiple cravings.
    • Prices are listed by each menu item, which gives the customers an idea of how much to bring or how much they want to spend.
    • Small description of the food item, such as what comes with it or what it is made with.

  • Popular Spot for events

    • Descriptions on seating arrangements for different parts of the venue.
    • Gives customers different package options with food that can be served along with prices.
    • Gives alternative menus of food and beverages for the event along with prices.

  • Online Presence

    • A lot of media presence and posts that help connect with their customers.
    • Shows accessibility with their customers letting them know they can be reached.
    • Allows them to show off their brand and showcase what they stand for.

  • Dated website and SEO

    • The layout of the website needs creativity such as breaking content into smaller parts.
    • The overall design is outdated and isn’t eye-catching.
    • Search engine keywords aren’t aligning with customers’ searches and lack customer reviews.

  • Unoptimized, out-of-date images

    • Size of images are a bit small to see a clear view of food items and tavern need to increase the size.
    • Images need to be up to date to show current tavern and food items.
    • Images give the website an outdated appearance.

  • Low customer engagement

    • The website has no visual engagement tools for customers.
    • Too much information on one page can overwhelm user’s eyes, so content needs to be broken down.
    • Only one social media platform on the website, which limits interaction with customers.
External Opportunities Threats

  • A new focus on take-out

    • Gives customers an option to dine-in or take out.
    • Follows quarantine regulations with the number of people that can be in the restaurant.
    • Another alternative for the customer to be able to take out food and meet friends or family somewhere else as meeting spots are at risk with COVID regulations.

  • Possible delivery options

    • Those who are unable to go out can have Tastey’s Taverns food to their home.
    • Provides an opportunity to start catering to events hosted in a business or home.
    • Keeps staff and customers safe as quarantine guidelines keep updating.

  • New photos of menu and interior

    • Gives menu items a boost to being purchased.
    • Allows first-time customers to get a glimpse of the interior of the tavern.
    • Provides those with disabilities to read to see what the company provides.

  • Quarantine Regulations

    • COVID Regulations will limit the capacity of which they can grow.
    • The business will have to spend more money on sanitization and cleaning supplies to keep everyone safe.
    • More in-depth cleaning processes will slow down the customer turnover rate.

  • Limited delivery options

    • Deliveries may require more time until delivered due to sanitation processes.
    • Increased packaging will cause an increase in time to be prepared until delivered.
    • Food Safety/Handling will need to show extra precaution to guarantee customer safety.
