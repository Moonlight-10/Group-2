SWOT Analysis

Internal Strengths Weaknesses

  • Well received menu

    • Different categories of food served that provide for multiple cravings.
    • Prices are listed by each menu item, which gives the customers an idea of how much to bring or how much they want to spend.
    • Small description of the food item, such as what comes with it or what it is made with.

  • Popular Spot for events

    • Descriptions on seating arrangements for different parts of the venue.
    • Offer different package options with food that can be served along with prices.
    • Alternative menus of food and beverages for the event along with prices.

  • Online Presence

    • Media presence and posts that help connect with their customers.
    • Recommend showing accessibility with their customers, letting them know they can be reached.
    • Allow the bar to show off their brand and showcase what they stand for.

  • Dated website and SEO

    • The layout of the website needs the content broken into smaller parts.
    • The overall design is not eye-catching, needs improvement in flow, color, and images.
    • Search engine keywords should align with potential customers’ searches.

  • Unoptimized images

    • Images are often too low-resolution or stock photos. Recommend taking new photos at a higher quality, and adding pictures of the more popular items on the menu.
    • Images need to be up to date to show current tavern and food items.
    • Images should give the website a colorful, eye-catching appearance.

  • Low customer engagement

    • The website has no engagement tools for customers. Recommend adding options to book, review, or follow on social media.
    • Online reviews are not responded to, needs active customer interaction to increase brand awareness.
    • Only one social media platform on the website, which limits interaction with customers. Adding an additional 2-3 should drive customers to stay interested.
External Opportunities Threats

  • A new focus on take-out

    • Encourage customers to order take out popular dishes using popular apps such as GrubHub, Postmates, UberEats, etc.
    • Follow quarantine regulations with the number of people that can be in the restaurant while promoting the most popular items.
    • Promote the food service side of the business, driving new customers to try the menu and encourage visits.

  • Possible delivery options

    • Those who are unable to go out can have Thirsty’s Tavern food to their home.
    • Provides an opportunity to start catering to events hosted in a business or home.
    • Keeps staff and customers safe as quarantine guidelines keep updating.

  • New photos of menu and interior

    • Give menu items a boost to being purchased by showing unique, in-house photos of the menu's most popular items.
    • Allow first-time customers to get an appealing glimpse of the interior of the tavern.
    • Provide those with disabilities to read and see how the company accommodates their needs.

  • Quarantine Regulations

    • COVID Regulations will limit the capacity of dine-in eating and drinking. Recommend pushing take-out and possible delivery options.
    • Thorough training on sanitation for staff members and cleaning supplies to keep everyone safe.
    • More in-depth cleaning processes will encourage wary potential customers to visit for in-house dining and drinks.

  • Limited delivery options

    • Thirsty's Tavern does not make any delivery options apparent on their website or social media, recommend adding CTA buttons, banners, or icons to drive interaction.
    • Delivery will allow staff to continue working through the pandemic to fulfill orders, should they so choose.
    • Delivery options will encourage new potential customers to try the menu during quarantine.

  • Competitive Environment

    • Location is home to many popular local dining spots, recommend boosting SEO to compete with surrounding competition.
    • Many chain restaurants are likely to draw in tourists or otherwise non-local customers. Improved online visibility or focus on local live entertainment may drive them to visit.
    • Thirsty's has lasted for decades in an infamously cutthroat business, continued focus on the business' mission in providing quality food and drinks with friendly service will ensure it continues to stand out.
